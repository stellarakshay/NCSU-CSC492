Connecting to Piazza will allow Concert to automatically monitor your students' activity on Piazza.

Option 1: Use your Piazza credential

You could give the your own piazza account id and password to Concert, we will automatically use that credential to access data from Piazza.

If this is the first time you add your piazza account to Concert, please click " Connect to a new account"

If you have added before and want to use that account, please click "connect to a previous account", and select the account you want to link with this course through the dropbox

Option 2: Use our credential

If you don't feel comfortable to share your own account, you can also add our piazza account into your course on piazza; then on the Concert side connect page, click connect to a default account. This action will allow Concert collecting piazza data through our piazza account. Please make sure it have the correct access on Piazza side.
